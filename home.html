Who are the Hellions?

The Outer Rim Hellions is a SWTOR guild founded on the principles of community, casual fun, and a love of Star Wars.

Weekly Events

The Hellions hold a series of weekly events including Operations, Flashpoints, Warzone, and others.

Conqeust Rewards

Conquest Rewards are given out on a weekly basis to those who contribute the most to the guild's conqeust level. The more you play, the more you win.
